Ticket Information

Cash Only

Please note that the Duquesne Incline accepts cash only. No credit or debit cards.

One-Way Fares

  • Adults: $5.00
  • Children (6-11): $2.50
  • Children (5 & under): Free
  • Seniors (65+): $2.50

Round-Trip Fares

  • Adults: $10.00
  • Children (6-11): $5.00
  • Children (5 & under): Free
  • Seniors (65+): $5.00

Getting Here

The lower station of the Duquesne Incline is located on West Carson Street at the base of Mount Washington. The upper station is at 1220 Grandview Avenue.
