Frequently Asked Questions

What are your hours of operation?

Monday - Saturday: 5:30 AM - 12:30 AM
Sunday: 7:00 AM - 12:30 AM

Do you accept credit cards?

No, the Duquesne Incline accepts cash only for tickets and gift shop purchases. There is an ATM available at the Upper Station.

Is the Duquesne Incline accessible?

Due to the historic nature of our facility, there are some accessibility limitations. Please contact us directly to discuss specific accommodations.

Is parking available?

Yes, there is a paid parking lot at the Lower Station on West Carson Street. Street parking is also available near the Upper Station on Grandview Avenue.

Can I make group reservations?

Yes, we accept group reservations for 10 or more people. Please fill out our Group Booking Inquiry form or contact us directly at (412) 381-1665.

Are pets allowed on the incline?

Service animals are welcome. Unfortunately, we cannot accommodate pets that are not service animals.
