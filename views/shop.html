Gift Shop Information

Location & Hours

Our gift shop is located in the Upper Station of the Duquesne Incline.

Hours: Same as incline operating hours

  • Monday - Saturday: 5:30 AM - 12:30 AM
  • Sunday: 7:00 AM - 12:30 AM

Payment Information

The gift shop accepts cash only. No credit or debit cards.

There is an ATM available at the Upper Station for your convenience.

Gift Shop Policies

  • All sales are final. No refunds or exchanges.
  • We cannot ship merchandise. All items must be purchased in person.
  • Larger items may be held at the gift shop until the end of your visit.
  • Product availability may vary. We cannot guarantee specific items will be in stock.

Group Shopping

For school groups and tours, we recommend allowing at least 15-20 minutes for gift shop browsing time. Teachers and group leaders receive a 10% discount on purchases.
